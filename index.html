Compelling design
for people, brands, and causes.

So White Design & Illustration is a boutique design studio based in Miami, Florida.

We create strategic solutions for the issues our clients face. Whether it’s branding, a website, a campaign, or all of the above, our design products are driven by a thoughtful, creative approach & a commitment to quality.

We love to work on projects that have a net-positive impact in society – creating design solutions that empower people and do good in the community.

Here’s what it looks like to work with us:

discover

The first step in every project is discovery.

Understanding your industry, clients and stakeholders, goals, and challenges is key. We take the time to really scope out your needs, so that we can build a product together that’s powerful and effective. We look at the kinds of things your users ask for and need. And we look for inspiration – things that are meaningful, remarkable, and surprising in what you offer, and how we can best connect those things to your users.

collaborate

For us, the design process is always collaborative. We rely on the wealth of knowledge, information, and experience that our clients have. We offer design concepts as drafts to be refined, challenged, and strengthened by working together. We consider open communication and iteration to be foundational to great design.
